About Me


We are constantly looking for self-motivated RAs (with Funding) to join HUMAN LAB! If interested, please don't hesitate to email me!

I am currently a Ph.D. student at University of Macau, advised by Prof. Zhenning Li. Previously, I received my Bachelor's degree from Chang'an University in 2021 and my Master's degree from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC) in 2022. Before joining UM, I was also fortunate to be mentored by Prof. Ruimin Ke and Prof. Ruey (Kelvin) Cheu.

I am broadly interested in AI for Transportation. My current research interests focus on Autonomous Driving and Transportation safety. On these topics, I have published papers as the first or co-first author in prestigious journals or AI conferences such as TPAMI, AAAI, Information Fusion, and AAP.
